About

A bit about me and what I do.

Who am I?

I'm IndieDave. I'm an Indie Game Developer and Content Creator. I've been developing games since I was a kid, and it's my main passion.

What do I do?

I mainly develop games, though I'm also into modelling, animating, web-development, Video-Editing, etc.

What are my goals?

My main goal is to entertain. Plain and simple.
I value simplicity over complexity in all of my projects.
I also share templates, tools and tips to help other developers.

The project was meant to become a full game, but I decided to keep it as a simple Demo for testing and reviewing purposes.
